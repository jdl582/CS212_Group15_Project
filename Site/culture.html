Flagstaff Celebrates Annual Lowell Observatory Star Party

Flagstaff’s annual Lowell Observatory Star Party brought together astronomy enthusiasts of all ages to explore the night sky and learn about space science. The event combines education, community engagement, and Flagstaff’s unique dark-sky advantage, inspiring curiosity and wonder in attendees.
